Biography

Henry Ije is a visionary problem solver and an budding software engineer and a data analyst. He is a fellow at Tiidelab and focuses on backend engineering track. He has three years of experience in the manufacturing industry and he wants to use his skills as a software engineer and data analyst to solve problems in the industry.

Henry studied Chemical engineering at Landmark University, Omu-Aran, Kwara State and during his undergraduate studies, he conducted a research on air pollution titled “The Investigation of Air pollution from combustion processes in Lagos, Nigeria.” His curious mind and passion for learning and courage to pursue unconventional paths lead him into technology.
